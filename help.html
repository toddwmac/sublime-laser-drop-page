Keeping this page current

A note for future me — I don't visit often.

This page refreshes itself. I don't edit it by hand.

  • To add something: tell my assistant a name and where it should point. Then sync.
  • To remove something: ask my assistant to take it down. It clears on its own. Then sync.
  • To rename or reorder: describe the change to my assistant. Then sync.

The only step that ever actually publishes anything is syncing. Everything else, I hand to my assistant and let the page take care of itself.
